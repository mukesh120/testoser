üöÄ Getting Started

What is AML verification? ‚ñº

AML (Anti-Money Laundering) verification is a process that checks if your cryptocurrency wallet and transactions comply with financial regulations. It helps ensure that your crypto assets are legitimate and not associated with illegal activities.

Our service analyzes your wallet's transaction history, assesses risk factors, and provides a compliance certificate that proves your assets meet regulatory standards.

How do I start the verification process? ‚ñº

Getting verified is simple and takes just a few minutes:

  • Step 1: Connect your crypto wallet (MetaMask or Trust Wallet)
  • Step 2: Approve USDT access for verification
  • Step 3: Wait 2-3 minutes for analysis
  • Step 4: Receive your security certificate

No personal information required - just your wallet address!

Is my wallet information secure? ‚ñº

Absolutely! Security is our top priority. We only analyze public blockchain data that's already available on the Ethereum network. We never:

  • Access your private keys
  • Store your seed phrase
  • Request personal information
  • Have control over your funds

Your wallet remains completely secure throughout the verification process.

What does the verification check for? ‚ñº

Our comprehensive verification analyzes:

  • Transaction history: Patterns and source of funds
  • Risk assessment: Connections to known risky addresses
  • Compliance check: AML/KYC regulatory standards
  • Blockchain verification: On-chain activity analysis
  • Security score: Overall wallet safety rating
How long does verification take? ‚ñº

The entire verification process takes approximately 2-3 minutes:

  • Wallet connection: 10-20 seconds
  • USDT approval: 30-60 seconds (gas transaction)
  • Analysis: 60-90 seconds
  • Certificate generation: Instant

You'll see real-time progress updates throughout the process.

What is the security score? ‚ñº

Your security score is a percentage (0-100%) that represents how well your wallet complies with AML standards. It's calculated based on:

  • Transaction legitimacy
  • Wallet age and activity
  • Risk factor assessment
  • Compliance with regulations

A score of 75% or higher is considered excellent and shows your assets are well-compliant.

üíª Technical Questions

Which wallets are supported? ‚ñº

We support all Ethereum-compatible wallets including:

  • MetaMask (desktop & mobile)
  • Trust Wallet (mobile)
  • Coinbase Wallet
  • WalletConnect compatible wallets
  • Any wallet that supports Ethereum Mainnet

Make sure you're connected to Ethereum Mainnet (not testnets).

Do I need to pay gas fees? ‚ñº

Yes, you'll need to pay a small gas fee (usually $3-8 in ETH) when approving USDT access. This is a one-time blockchain transaction fee that goes to Ethereum miners, not to us.

Important: Make sure you have enough ETH in your wallet to cover gas fees before starting verification.

What if I don't have USDT in my wallet? ‚ñº

You don't need to own USDT to complete verification! We only request approval to access USDT data for analysis purposes. The verification process works even if your USDT balance is zero.

However, you'll still need a small amount of ETH for gas fees.

üìú Certificate & Results

What do I receive after verification? ‚ñº

After successful verification, you'll receive:

  • Digital Certificate: Professional AML compliance certificate
  • Security Score: Your compliance rating (0-100%)
  • Certificate ID: Unique verification identifier
  • Timestamp: Verification date and time
  • Downloadable Report: JSON data of your verification
How long is the certificate valid? ‚ñº

Your AML verification certificate is valid for 90 days from the date of issuance. After 90 days, we recommend re-verifying to ensure your wallet maintains compliance with the latest regulations.

You can verify your wallet as many times as needed - there's no limit!

Can I download my certificate? ‚ñº

Yes! You can download your verification data in JSON format. The certificate includes all verification details and can be:

  • Saved for your records
  • Shared with exchanges or platforms
  • Used as proof of compliance
  • Re-accessed anytime through "View Certificate"
